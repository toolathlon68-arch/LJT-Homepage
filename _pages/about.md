# About Me

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.ust.hk/), supervised by [Professor Junxian He](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~jhe/). I graduated from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree.

My research focuses on **natural language processing and machine learning**, with specific interests in:
- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM truthfulness and Interpretability
